## Data-layer quality issues detected

The main structural risk I see is **conflating national norm with local adoption**. The official GALA, IZA, AZWA and mental-health-network pages are much stronger norm sources than partner summaries, but they still only answer what the agreements require nationally or regionally. They do not, by themselves, prove that Almere has adopted a given basisfunctionaliteit or D6 component. ţłÇciteţłéturn40view0ţłéturn41view0ţłéturn12view0ţłéturn39view0ţłü

A second risk is **conflating regional planning with municipal ownership**.
